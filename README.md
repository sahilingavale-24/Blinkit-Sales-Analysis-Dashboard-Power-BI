# Blinkit-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI
Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, outlet operations. The project aims to track key KPIs such as total sales, average sales, item count, and ratings, while identifying trends across product categories, outlet size, locations, and types to support data-driven business decisions.

## Dataset used
- <a href="https://github.com/sahilingavale-24/Blinkit-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI/blob/main/BlinkIT%20Grocery%20Data.xlsx">Dataset</a>


## KPI's Requirements
Total Sales: The overall revenue generated from all items sold.

Average Sales: The average revenue per sale.

Number of Items: The total count of different items sold.

Average Rating: The average customer rating for items sold.

## Process
Import & Clean Data
Load the Blinkit dataset into Power BI.
Remove duplicates, handle missing values, and correct data types using Power Query.
Create KPIs
Build DAX measures for:
Total Sales
Average Sales
Number of Items
Average Rating
Design Visualizations
Create charts for:
Outlet Establishment Trend
Fat Content Analysis
Item Type Sales
Outlet Size & Location Analysis
Outlet Type Performance
Add Filters & Interactivity
Insert slicers for Outlet Location, Outlet Size, and Item Type.
Enable cross-filtering between visuals for interactive analysis.
Format & Publish Dashboard
Apply Blinkit-themed colors and layout.
Add titles, labels, and KPI cards.
Publish the dashboard to Power BI Service and share insights.

## Project Insight
1. Overall Business Performance
Total Sales: $1.20M generated across all products.
Average Sales per Transaction: $141.
Total Items Sold: 8,523.
Average Customer Rating: 3.9/5, indicating generally positive customer satisfaction.
2. Outlet Establishment Trends
Sales showed steady growth from 2012 to 2017.
2018 recorded the highest sales ($205K), making it the best-performing year.
Sales slightly declined after 2018 but remained stable above $129K annually.
3. Product Category Performance
Fruits & Vegetables ($0.18M) and Snack Foods ($0.18M) are the top-selling categories.
Household items ($0.14M) and Frozen Foods ($0.12M) also contribute significantly.
Seafood and Breakfast products generate the lowest sales, indicating potential areas for improvement.
4. Fat Content Analysis
Low-Fat products ($776.32K) contribute significantly more revenue than Regular products ($425.36K).
Customer demand appears stronger for healthier product options.
5. Outlet Size Analysis
Medium-sized outlets generate the highest revenue ($507.90K).
Small outlets contribute $444.79K.
High-sized outlets contribute $248.99K, making them the lowest-performing outlet size.
6. Outlet Location Performance
Tier 3 locations lead sales with $472.13K.
Tier 2 locations generate $393.15K.
Tier 1 locations contribute $336.40K.
Expansion opportunities appear strongest in Tier 3 markets.
7. Outlet Type Performance
Supermarket Type 1 is the best-performing outlet type:
Total Sales: $787.55K
Items Sold: 5,577
Average Sales: $141
Grocery Stores generate lower overall sales despite similar ratings.
All outlet types maintain a consistent average rating of 4, reflecting stable customer satisfaction.


## Final Conclusion:
Blinkit's strongest performance comes from Tier 3 locations, Medium-sized outlets, and Supermarket Type 1 stores. Customer preference is higher for Low-Fat products, while Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the top revenue-generating categories. To drive future growth, Blinkit should focus on expanding successful outlet formats, strengthening high-demand product categories, and improving sales in underperforming categories such as Seafood and Breakfast items.
